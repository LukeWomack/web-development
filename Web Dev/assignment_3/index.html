Will Hunting is a brilliant but troubled young man from South Boston. He's a janitor at MIT, but secretly has a god-given gift for solving complex mathematical problems. He's got a rebellious streak and a troubled past, but beneath the tough exterior lies a kind and sensitive soul. Will's journey of self-discovery and finding love with Skylar is truly inspiring, any time I need a pick-me-up, I turn on Good Will Hunting.
